About MIBSP

MIBSP (Municipal Integrity & Bribe-Free Service Portal) is a secure grievance platform that lets citizens report corruption and service issues without creating an account.

Anonymous by Design

Citizens can submit complaints without login or registration.

Transparent Tracking

Each complaint receives a tracking ID for status visibility.

Accountable Workflow

Role-based review with audit trails for administrative actions.

Core Objectives

  • Reduce barriers to reporting misconduct.
  • Improve redressal speed across departments.
  • Provide measurable public transparency.
