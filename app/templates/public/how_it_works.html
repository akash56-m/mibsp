How The Portal Works

A simple four-step process for submitting and tracking municipal grievances.

1. Submit

Choose department and service, then describe the issue.

2. Track

Save your tracking ID shown after successful submission.

3. Review

Authorized officers investigate and update complaint status.

4. Resolve

Track progress until action is taken and complaint is closed.

Important: Keep your tracking ID safe. The system cannot recover lost IDs.
